Pumpkin Pie

    Ingredients

  • 1 Refrigerated Pie Crust
  • 1 15oz Can Pumpkin
  • 2 Eggs
  • 3/4 Cup White Sugar
  • 1 Teaspoon Ground Cinnamon
  • 1/2 Teaspoon Table Salt
  • 1/2 Teaspoon Ground Ginger
  • 1/4 Teaspoon Ground Cloves

    Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 425°F. Mix sugar, cinnamon, salt, ginger, and cloves in a small bow. Beat the eggs in a large bowl. Stir in the canned pumpkin and the sugar-spice mixture. Gradaully stir in the milk.
  2. Roll the pie crust into a 9" pan, trim and crimp the edge. Pour the pumpkin mixture into the prepared crust.
  3. Bake at 425°F for 15 minutes. Reduce oven temperature to 350°F and make for 40 minutes or until knife inserted near the center comes out clean.
  4. Cool on wire rack for 2 hours before serving. Refrigerate any leftovers.
